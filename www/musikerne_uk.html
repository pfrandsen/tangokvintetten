Bjarne Hansen, violin - renowned soloist and chamber musician both in Denmark and abroad. Concert master of the Odense Symphony Orchestra for more than twenty years, currently concert master at The Royal Danish Orchestra in Copenhagen.
Ole Bartholin Kiilerich, piano - versatile soloist, performer and improviser with a broad range of repertoire who appears on numerous recordings of contemporary music.
Jørgen Ehlers, guitar - associate professor at the The Carl Nielsen Academy of Music in Odense who is also an active musician and composer with several recordings in his own name over the years.
Jens Krøgholt, double bass - double bass player at Odense Symphony Orchestra who feels equally comfortable in both jazz and folk, and of course tango.
Branko Djordjevic, accordeon – price winner at numerous music competitions around Europe. A virtuoso at concerts in many genres such as classical, klezmer, folk, and of course tango.
